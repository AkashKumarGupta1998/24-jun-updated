About Me

From Chemistry Labs to Control Towers

I'm an operations professional with a unique journey — from teaching Chemistry & Mathematics for 5+ years to leading control tower operations for India's top quick-commerce platforms.

I combine analytical rigour with on-ground operational instinct — translating real-time data into decisions that move the needle.
